Frequently Asked Questions

Is this platform free?

Yes, completely free. We aggregate public RSS feeds of Nepali audiobooks.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to search, listen, or download.

Do you host the audio files?

No, we do not host any files. The audio plays directly from the original creators' hosting platforms via public RSS feeds.
